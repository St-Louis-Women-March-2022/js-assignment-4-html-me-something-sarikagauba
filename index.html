Cooking Is My Passion

There are many things that bring joy to me, but i feel that cooking is my passion. Cooking is pure joy and fun for me. Anything I cook makes me feel good.
Cooking is my time to be me. My family is excited when I cook because they know it will be exciting and interesting.
My passion for cooking blossomed after marriage. My husband rarely ate home cooked food before marriage.
That was a big motivation for me to develop love for cooking. In a short time I started to cook vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes.
The motivation of cooking non-veg dishes goes to my two boys because I am pure vegitarian but my boys and husband loves non-vegetarian food.

My Favourite cooked vegetarian and Non-Vegetarian dishes

  • Aloo Poori

    • Aloo Poori is favorite breakfast with some chopped onions & pickle.
    Here aloo refers to potato curry & puri is Indian fried bread.
    Since both these dishes are served together, it named as aloo puri.

  • Chole Bhature

    • Chole Bhature is the most popular Punjabi dish.
    Chole stands for a spiced tangy chickpea and Bhatura is a soft & fluffy fried leavened bread.

  • Dal Makhani

    • Dal Makhani is a delicious & creamy punjabi dish which is served with Naan or Chapati.
    It is mix of whole black dal urad lentils & red kidney beans.

  • Butter chicken & Naan

    • Butter Chicken consists mainly of chunks of boneless, skinless cooked chicken in a spicy tomato sauce made from tomatoes and butter.
    Butter chicken is typically served with rice or naan bread.

  • Curry Fish & Rice

    • Tomato Fish Curry is a delicious Indian curry where fish pieces are cooked in a spicy tomato based gravy.
    This fish curry tastes best with plain steamed rice.
